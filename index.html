WHO WE ARE

We are not
a company.

KOPIT is a registered technology cooperative — where every member is a co-owner, every project builds shared wealth, and every ringgit earned comes back to the people who earned it.

We are not investors waiting for returns. We are not employees collecting salaries. We are builders, owners, and partners — quietly building Malaysia's digital and energy future, one project at a time.
