THE PHILOSOPHY

Curated by the cooperative.

KOPIT does not chase membership numbers. We choose people who believe what we believe — that wealth, when built collectively, returns to those who built it.

Every application — from the first share at RM 200 to the strategic partnership of RM 1,000,000 — is reviewed by the Board of Directors. Approval is not transactional. It is a judgment of alignment.

GOVERNANCE PILLAR

The Board Is Not A Membership Tier.

The BOD governs the cooperative. Elected at AGM by members. You cannot apply for this — but every application passes through them.

CAPITAL STRUCTURE

Two Streams. One Member.

Every member holds shares in the cooperative. Some choose to do more — extending capital to KOPIT under our SKM-approved Capital Investment Programme.

STREAM ONE
Share Capital
Modal Saham · Permanent Equity
VALUE PER SHARE RM 200
RETURNS
Annual Dividend
Declared at AGM based on cooperative profit.
SHARE QUANTITY BY TIER
  • ◆ KB — RM 200 minimum (1 share)
  • ◆ KP / KL — Higher quantity, BOD-determined
  • ◆ KI1 / KI2 / KI3 — Min 2% of investment value
OPTIONAL
STREAM TWO
Capital Investment Programme
Pelaburan Modal · Fixed Return Arrangement
MINIMUM RM 10,000
RETURNS Competitive Fixed Return
RATE BOD-Determined
TERM
Up to 3 years
After term: auto-renew at BOD-reviewed rate, or capital returned to member.
VOLUME RECLASSIFIES TIER
  • ◆ RM 10K – 100K → KI1 — Entry Partner
  • ◆ RM 100K – 300K → KI2 — Growth Partner
  • ◆ RM 300K – 1M → KI3 — Strategic Partner
